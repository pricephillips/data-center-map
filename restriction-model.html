Reading rule. Associations are predictive, not causal. The calibrated score is an out-of-fold model probability that a county has an enacted restrictive data center policy (moratorium or restrictive zoning with terminal enacted status) given its profile. It is not a forecast of opposition to any specific project. County-specific opposition rates are not computable at current data density.
